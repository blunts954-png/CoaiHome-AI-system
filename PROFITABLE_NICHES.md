## Niches to AVOID

❌ Electronics (warranty issues, high returns)
❌ Clothing (sizing issues, 30%+ return rate)
❌ Shoes (same as clothing)
❌ Fragile items (shipping damage)
❌ Oversized items (high shipping costs)
❌ Copyrighted products (legal issues)
❌ Supplements (FDA regulations)
❌ Toys (safety regulations)

## Niche Selection Criteria

✅ Checklist before choosing:
- [ ] Can I demonstrate it in a 15-30 sec video?
- [ ] Does it solve a specific problem?
- [ ] Can I sell it for 2.5-3x cost?
- [ ] Is shipping under 14 days?
- [ ] Is the market big but not saturated?
- [ ] Do people buy emotionally or logically?
- [ ] Is it easy to understand without explanation?
