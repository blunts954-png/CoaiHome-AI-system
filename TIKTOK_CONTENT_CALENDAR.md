## Content Creation Tips

### Filming Setup (Under $50):
- Phone tripod: $15
- Ring light: $25
- Phone (you already have): $0
- Clean background (white wall or organized space): $0

### Best Times to Post (EST):
- Morning: 8:00-9:00 AM
- Lunch: 12:00-1:00 PM
- Evening: 7:00-9:00 PM

### Video Specs:
- Format: 9:16 (vertical)
- Length: 15-30 seconds (optimal)
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Text overlay for accessibility
- Trending audio
- Call to action at end

### Engagement Hacks:
- Reply to EVERY comment in first hour
- Use trending sounds within 24-48 hours
- Post consistently (2-3x daily)
- Engage with other organization accounts
- Use 5-7 hashtags
- Pin best performing video to profile
