### 1. TikTok Account Setup
- Create a **TikTok Business Account** (free)
- Switch to Pro/Business mode in settings
- Link your Shopify store in bio

### 2. Equipment Needed
- **Smartphone** (iPhone or Android)
- **Ring light** (optional but recommended - $15-30 on Amazon)
- **Tripod or phone stand** ($10-20)
- **Clean background** (white wall or organized space)

### 3. TikTok Content the System Generates

When you click "Generate TikTok Content", the system creates:

```
📁 content_calendar.csv

For each product, you get:
- Video Title/Hook (first 3 seconds grab attention)
- Script (what to say/show)
- Caption (text overlay + description)
- Hashtags (trending + niche)
- Call-to-Action ("Link in bio!" / "Shop now!")
- Best posting time
```

## Video Types That Work for Home Organization

### 1. "Before & After" Transformation (BEST PERFORMER)
**Hook:** "POV: You finally organize your kitchen"
- Show messy drawer/cabinet
- Quick transition
- Show organized space with your product
- **Result:** High engagement, emotional response

### 2. "Satisfying Restock" (VIRAL POTENTIAL)
**Hook:** "Restocking my pantry is my therapy"
- Empty the organizer
- Refill with colorful items
- ASMR-style sounds
- **Result:** High saves/shares

### 3. "Problem → Solution" (BEST FOR SALES)
**Hook:** "Tired of messy cables everywhere?"
- Show the problem (messy cables)
- Introduce product (cable organizer)
- Show organized result
- **Result:** Direct conversions

### 4. "Day in the Life" (PERSONAL CONNECTION)
**Hook:** "Organizing my bathroom with finds from TikTok"
- Real person using product
- Natural, authentic feel
- **Result:** Trust building

### 5. "Quick Tip/Hack" (EDUCATIONAL)
**Hook:** "This $25 organizer changed my life"
- Quick demo
- Highlight key benefit
- **Result:** High engagement

## Posting Strategy (The System's Schedule)

### Week 1: Launch Content
- **Day 1:** Store intro video (who you are)
- **Day 2-7:** Post 1 product video per day

### Week 2+: Consistency
- **Daily:** 1-2 videos per day
- **Mix:** 60% product demos, 40% lifestyle/behind-scenes
- **Best times:** 7-9 AM, 12-1 PM, 7-9 PM (your timezone)

### The 80/20 Rule
- **80%** Educational/Entertaining (builds audience)
- **20%** Direct sales (link in bio)

## Step-by-Step: Making Your First Video

### Step 1: Generate Content Ideas
1. Go to dashboard: http://localhost:8000
2. Click **"Generate TikTok Content"**
3. Wait for CSV file to be created
4. Open `content_calendar.csv`

### Step 2: Choose a Product
Start with your highest-margin product:
- Drawer Organizer (67.4% margin)
- Bathroom Caddy (66.7% margin)

### Step 3: Film (Using System Script)
Example script for "Drawer Organizer":
```
HOOK (0-3 sec): "My drawer was chaos until I found this"
[Show messy drawer]

BODY (3-20 sec):
"This expandable organizer fits ANY drawer"
[Show it expanding]
"Now everything has a place"
[Show organized result]

CTA (20-30 sec):
"Link in bio - only $22!"
[Show product + your hand pointing up]
```

### Step 4: Edit in TikTok
- Add trending sound (search "trending sounds 2024")
- Add text overlays
- Speed up boring parts (2x speed for transitions)

### Step 5: Post
- Use system-generated hashtags
- Add "Link in bio" in caption
- Post at recommended time
- Pin comment with link
