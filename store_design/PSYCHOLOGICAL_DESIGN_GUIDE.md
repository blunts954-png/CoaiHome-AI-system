## COLOR PSYCHOLOGY FOR CONVERSIONS

### Primary Color Scheme: "Trust & Action"

| Color | Hex Code | Psychology | Usage |
|-------|----------|------------|-------|
| **Trust Blue** | `#2563EB` | Trust, security, calm | Headers, logos, primary buttons |
| **Action Orange** | `#F97316` | Urgency, excitement, CTA | Buy buttons, sale badges, urgency |
| **Money Green** | `#10B981` | Success, savings, go | Checkout buttons, savings displays |
| **Luxury Black** | `#1A1A2E` | Authority, premium | Text, luxury elements |
| **Clean White** | `#FFFFFF` | Space, clarity | Backgrounds |
| **Soft Gray** | `#F5F5F5` | Neutrality | Sections, cards |

### Why These Colors Work:

**Blue (#2563EB) - The Trust Builder**
- Used by: PayPal, Facebook, IBM
- Effect: Increases trust by 34%
- Use: Logo, header, primary navigation

**Orange (#F97316) - The Action Driver**
- Used by: Amazon (Add to Cart), Home Depot
- Effect: Creates urgency without panic
- Use: "Add to Cart", "Buy Now", countdown timers

**Green (#10B981) - The Money Signal**
- Used by: Starbucks, Whole Foods
- Effect: Associated with savings & success
- Use: Checkout, "You saved $X", free shipping

## PSYCHOLOGICAL COPYWRITING

### Words That Convert:

| Instead Of | Use This | Why It Works |
|------------|----------|--------------|
| "Buy Now" | "Get My Organizer" | Ownership transfer |
| "Sale" | "Limited Time: 40% Off" | Scarcity + specificity |
| "Cheap" | "Affordable Luxury" | Reframes value |
| "Organize" | "Transform Your Space" | Emotional outcome |
| "Product" | "Solution" | Problem-solving frame |
| "$29.99" | "Only $29.99" | Minimizes price perception |
