## How to Choose Your Name

### Checklist:
- [ ] Easy to spell when heard
- [ ] Easy to pronounce when read
- [ ] Less than 15 characters
- [ ] No numbers or hyphens (cleaner)
- [ ] .com or .co available (or .shop, .store)
- [ ] Not trademarked (check uspto.gov)
- [ ] Instagram handle available
- [ ] TikTok handle available

### Quick Test:
1. Say it out loud 5 times
2. Tell someone over the phone, ask them to spell it
3. Type it fast - did you make typos?
4. Google it - any bad associations?
5. Check domain availability (Namecheap, GoDaddy)

## What I Recommend for YOU

### If this is your first store:
**Go with a NICHE-SPECIFIC name**
- Easier to rank in SEO
- Customers know what you sell immediately
- Builds trust faster
- Easier content marketing

**Example:**
- Niche: Home Organization
- Name: **The Organized Home** or **Simplify Co**

### If you plan to pivot niches:
**Go with a GENERIC name**
- Can change products without rebranding
- More flexibility
- Harder to rank initially

**Example:**
- Name: **The Curated Co** or **Found & Co**

### If you want to eventually sell the AI tool:
**Go with an AI/TECH name**
- Positions you as tech-forward
- Can pivot to SaaS later
- Unique angle

**Example:**
- Name: **AutoFinds** or **SmartShelf**
